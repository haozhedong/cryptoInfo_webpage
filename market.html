Information about Bitcoin Market

Bitcoin, the pioneering cryptocurrency, has experienced a roller-coaster journey since its inception. Currently, the Bitcoin market is characterized by high volatility, with its price experiencing significant fluctuations influenced by market sentiment, regulatory news, and macroeconomic factors. Bitcoin reached an all-time high of nearly $69,000 in November 2021, but has also faced substantial crashes, such as the dramatic dip in 2018 and the steep decline in mid-2022, where it fell to around $20,000.
